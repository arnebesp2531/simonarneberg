Well, Here We Are

If you're reading this (which you are), then you already have a pretty good indication of what this website is like. I created simonarneberg.com in my free time after being introduced to Claude Code at a work-related workshop, and I was so intrigued by the world of possibilities it opened that I had to try my hand at making a personal website with it. I don't easily fall into the hype surrounding generative AI tools, but Claude honestly feels like one of the most game-changing tools I've worked with in a long time.

The Future of simonarneberg.com

I'm hoping to continually improve this site as I learn more, and as I have more ideas.
