About Me

Simon Arneberg
Data engineer by day, musician by night— I blend analytical thinking with creative expression.

I'm a data engineer at InterWorks, a people-focused tech consultancy. I build data pipelines, design analytics systems, and help organizations transform their data into actionable insights.

Outside of my professional work, I'm a musician creating original music under the name Simon Paul Arneberg. I've released 33 songs across 6 EPs, blending piano and trumpet to create atmospheric, minimalist compositions. I've been making music for over a decade.

I studied at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire with a focus on computer science and mathematics. I'm passionate about finding creative ways to visualize complex data and expressing ideas through both code and music.
