Music

I like to write and record original songs for fun. Since 2020, I've released 33 songs over 6 EPs.

Why do I write music?

For me, playing music is soothing, and I started writing music that I wanted to listen to. Usually that means a soft, indie-folk vibe, but I'll branch out stylistically every once in a while. I really enjoy when songs explore themes such as growth, change, and stillness. As such, my songs often reflect the stage of life I'm in.

My tools:

  • Piano -- my first instrumental love. Nothing is more soothing than playing ultra-repetitive compositions on a piano at 3am.
  • Guitar -- got my first one after graduating high school (thanks, siblings). Favorite tool for songwriting.
  • Trumpet -- played in wide variety of bands through college, concert band or jazz was probably my favorite. 2nd/3rd part preferred.
  • Bass -- only one note at a time? Sign me up!
  • Ukulele/etc -- love me a good midnight uke session on a warm summer night.
  • Vocals -- honestly, I still don't love the sound of my own voice, so I do my best to mask it with as many layers of harmony as possible.
